What is Weedout?

Weedout is a powerful and user-friendly data preprocessing library designed to streamline and enhance your machine learning workflow. Weedout automates the tedious tasks of data cleaning, transformation, and feature engineering, freeing you to focus on model development and insights generation. Whether you're working with cross-sectional data or time series, for classification or regression tasks, Weedout provides the essential tools to prepare your data efficiently and effectively. WeedOut prioritizes organizing your data and removing irrelevant information to ensure it is clean, organized, and accurate. This foundation aims at improving the performance of your machine learning models and enhances data-driven decision-making.
Note ** The current version of Weedout is designed to handle sampling datasets for two specific types of machine learning models: Binary Classification and Regression. As the project is still in its early stages, the sampling in the preprocessing pipeline is limited to these dataset types.
